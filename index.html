About Megan

Megan Carelse is a multi-talented artist and actor based in Johannesburg, South Africa. Blending deep soulful vocals with atmospheric songwriting, her music is an immersive journey into emotion and cinematic warmth.

A true storyteller, Megan is also recognized for her compelling screen acting credentials in drama and suspense thriller productions. Her work balances artistic vulnerability with raw, magnetic presence.
