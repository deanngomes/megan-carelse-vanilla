Biography

A Soul Bound to Storytelling

Megan Carelse is an independent artist, lyricist, and actor based out of the vibrant cultural landscape of Johannesburg, South Africa. Combining rich atmospheric songwriting with an magnetic vocal presence, she constructs musical worlds that feel deeply intimate yet vast and cinematic.

Johannesburg Foundations

Growing up amidst the energetic rhythm and creative diversity of Johannesburg, Megan was influenced early on by the power of raw storytelling. The local artistic community taught her to see art not just as entertainment, but as an essential dialogue. Her music reflects this grit and honesty—layered vocal arrangements and organic instrumentation echoing the vast emotional currents of city life and quiet winter introspection.

The Acoustic & Ambient Journey

Megan’s musical style represents a modern evolution of acoustic folk blended with ambient pop. Every release is treated as a narrative project. Her popular singles, such as "Peonies" and "My Gravity", capture milestones of her growth. "Peonies" tells the story of patient growth and waiting for things to bloom on their own schedule during a long dry highveld season. Her melodies rely heavily on layered warm vocals, creating a comforting blanket of sound for the listener.

The Cinematic Perspective

In addition to her primary focus on songwriting and music releases, Megan maintains professional credibility on screen. Her acting credentials in various South African theater, independent cinema, and dramatic productions add an incredible cinematic weight to her vocal delivery. Her understanding of pacing, subtext, and physical expression is evident in her live performances and music visualizers. For Megan, music and acting are simply two branches of the same tree: the pursuit of emotional truth.

"Storytelling is how we make sense of our gravity. It connects the songs we write to the scenes we inhabit, keeping us grounded in what truly matters."
