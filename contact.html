Store & Booking FAQs

Quick answers to common inquiries regarding digital single downloads, merchandise shipping, and live shows.

How do I receive my purchased digital tracks?

Once your payment is processed securely via our Stripe integration, you will immediately receive a secure email containing high-quality digital download links (.wav and 320kbps MP3 formats) along with your transaction receipt.

What is the shipping protocol for physical merchandise?

All garments are crafted with organic, sustainable fibers and shipped using eco-friendly carbon-neutral carrier logistics. Orders within South Africa are delivered within 3-5 business days. International shipping timelines vary between 7-14 days. Shipping is free for orders totaling R1000 or more.

Are live performances and private bookings supported?

Yes, Megan Carelse regularly performs acoustic sets, festival panels, and collaborative ambient sets. For booking availability, stage setup requirements, and financial quotes, please fill out the contact form with your event requirements.
